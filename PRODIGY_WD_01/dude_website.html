THE STARTING PHASE

The YouTube channel Where's My Challenge" was initially started by 'Matt' and 'Paul' on 15th of April in 2010 and then the team was quickly expanded to 4 people- Matt, Paul, Harry and Lewis. The channel has amassed a total of more than 150 million views across Social media platforms, getting more than 98 million views on YouTube alone. The channel is based in UK. This channel used to feature extreme challenges mainly revolving around food but not limited to it. There was something very special vibe about the channel that attracted viewers in a way that other channels failed to. The friendly banter, or the different extreme challenges God knows but if at that time if you wanted to see food-challenges ‘DWMW’ is where you went.

What happened?

Well until late 2016 everything was going fine for the DWMC guys. They were living the dream of their life as they described it. But in the start of 2017, the great YouTube Adpocalypse happened. In February 2017, PewDiePie, the most subscribed YouTuber at the time, came under fire for posting videos that YouTube deemed "Anti-Semitic" and "hate-speech". Other extremist content was also surfacing, that lead the UK Government, Coca-Cola, Dr. Pepper, Johnson & Johnson, and many major brands to pull their adverts from YouTube. This resulted in a lot of Content-Creators losing as much as 90% of the revenue and among those content-creators DWMC was also present. The channel lost as much as 95% of the ad-revenue overnight. Adding to the problem, about 60-70 strikes were made against the channel by some media-company resulting in the deletion of channel not once but twice. As if that wasn't enough, one of the guys from the team- Paul got terribly sick. He got infected from a bowel disease known as Crohn’s disease and had a near death experience.

Where are they now?

Paul married love of his life, Stephanie Parker on June 18, 2016. Now Paul has been blessed with three kids. Lewis left the group a little while after the Adpocalypse hit. Since then he has a full-time job. Though he regularly recordes podcasts with Matt on the channel ThreeSkinsPodcast, he remains absent on the main channel to this date. Matt is currently single and still spending his life in his own weird, funky, funny way. Harry too has a full-time job. But once a while these old men do put up videos on the main channel.
